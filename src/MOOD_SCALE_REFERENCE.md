## 💡 **How to Use:**

### **When Writing Diary Entry:**

1. Write your thoughts in the editor
2. Select your current mood from the dropdown
3. The mood will be saved with value 1-5
4. This appears in your weekly mood tracker

### **Viewing Mood Trends:**

1. Go to **Dashboard**
2. See **Weekly Mood Tracker** section
3. Each day shows:
   - Day name (Mon, Tue, etc.)
   - Mood value (1-5)
   - Color-coded bar
   - Mood label (Happy, Good, etc.)
4. Bottom shows **Weekly Average**

### **Understanding Your Mood:**

- **Mostly 4-5:** You're doing great! 🌟
- **Mostly 3:** You're balanced, consider activities to boost mood
- **Mostly 2:** Consider self-care activities or talking to someone
- **Mostly 1:** Please reach out for support - you deserve help! 💚
